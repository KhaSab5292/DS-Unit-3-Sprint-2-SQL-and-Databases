In the Northwind database, what is the type of relationship between the Employee and Territory tables?
The relationship is one to one. 	

What is a situation where a document store (like MongoDB) is appropriate, and what is a situation where it is not appropriate?
Appropriate when processing multi-document transactions.  

What is "NewSQL", and what is it trying to achieve?
NewSQL is a new class of relational database management systems created to achieve the  same scalability as NoSQL systems for online transaction processing workloads.
